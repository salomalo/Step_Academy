THE SALT OF “THE EARTH” IN RIGA: PRESENTATION OF NEW ALBUM OF OKEAN ELZY

In 2013, Ukrainian rock-legends recorded new album “The Earth” and went to great stadia tour to cities of the Ukraine, Russia and Belarus to offer new programme. On March 29, Okean Elzy will perform in concert hall“Palladium Riga”.

Everything that could be said about Okean Elzy may be considered as exclusive: exclusive emotions, exclusive energy, and exclusive openness. “If Vakarchuk writes a ballade, then it will make us to cry tears, if he writes a rock hymn, then it will make us to tear our shirts and jump up to the sky”, Oleg Martishin, musical journalist, wrote. This group has been called the main Ukrainian energy source. Hits sung by them will be enough for nearest century, but Okean Elzy would not have been Slavic Muse, Blur and Pulp altogether, if they hadn’t recorded new disc once in two years. “The Earth” became the eighth studio album and actually – the national-scale event in the Ukraine.

The album contains twelve songs, including single “Wall”, which is known to public within by-project “Brussels” of Svjatoslav Vakarchuk. Some songs root into listener’s consciousness immediately, and some songs need time to put down.
